TIPS & TRICKS

Having a hard time winning? Here are some tips & tricks for you!

1. Master the timing

Practice tapping at the right moment to keep the panda flying smoothly between obstacles. Consistent timing is key to success.





2. Stay calm and focused

Avoid getting frustrated and stay focused on the game. Panicking can lead to mistimed taps and crashes.




3. Study the patterns

Observe the patterns and learn when to tap to navigate through them effectively. Knowing the patterns will help you anticipate and react faster.

4. Use short taps

Instead of holding down for long taps, use short and quick taps to adjust the panda’s height more precisely, especially when navigating tight spaces.




5. Change your fly pattern

Persevere and eventually you'll reach the finish line! Just keep on trying it over and over until you find something that clicks.




6. Take breaks

Don’t forget to take breaks to rest your eyes and mind. Playing continously for too long can lead to fatigue and decreased
performance.
